About

A practical bridge between health systems, technology, governance, and delivery.

I support digital health strategy and transformation work with a focus on portfolio stewardship, reporting, financial visibility, contract coordination, and cross-organizational delivery support.

My earlier experience in U.S. Medicaid enterprise health IT shaped how I think about systems: technology needs to be usable, interoperable, responsibly governed, and aligned with real operational and clinical workflows.

I am advancing this work through the University of Toronto’s Executive Master of Health Informatics, with interests in Generative AI in health, evaluation, benefits realization, interoperability, and informatics leadership.
